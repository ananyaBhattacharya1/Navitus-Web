About Us

Navitus Controls & Equipment Private Limited (NCEPL) is a niche Service Provider that focuses on delivering end to end Automation Solutions for various Industrial Verticals. NCEPL has partnered with World leading Companies to deliver state-of-the-art Solutions in the areas of Industrial Process Automation and Real Time Condition Monitoring of Electrical assets.

NCEPL is a System Integrator that brings the right blend of Hardware and Software Platforms to yield the best Price Performance for its clients in the areas of Consulting Services and Systems Integration Projects. NCEPL currently focuses on Utility, Oil & Gas, Metal and Mining Verticals in key areas of :


  • Asset Management
  • Business Innovation
  • System Improvement
  • Productivity Enhancement.

NCEPL also provides technology and services that help its customers address the limitations of an old and aging Electrical infrastructure. NCEPL has partnered with leading manufacturers of Monitoring Devices, Communication Infrastructure Providers and Analytical Software Providers to design and configure end-to-end solution in the areas of “Condition Monitoring” of the Electrical Assets. Combining deep industry experience and technical knowhow with creative ideas, NCEPL has created intelligent, reliable and leading-edge technology solutions that add value to our customers’ businesses and help them to move to a “Condition Based Maintenance Program” from an existing “Time Based Maintenance Program”. Some of the benefits that our solutions can provide are :


  • Lowering the cost of maintenance of the electrical assets by introducing “Condition Based Maintenance” Program
  • Extending the life of the assets
  • Providing early alarm about the health of the assets and reducing the possibilities of unplanned outages and potential revenue loss

To provide solutions to our customers with HIGH QUALITY & MAINTENANCE FREE products in order to enhance their profitability and value.
