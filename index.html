Welcome to Navitus

Navitus Controls & Equipment Private Limited (NCEPL) is a niche Service Provider that focuses on delivering end to end Automation Solutions for various Industrial Verticals. NCEPL has partnered with World leading Companies to deliver state-of-the-art Solutions in the areas of Industrial Process Automation and Real Time Condition Monitoring of Electrical assets.NCEPL also provides technology and services that help its customers address the limitations of an old and aging Electrical infrastructure. NCEPL has partnered with leading manufacturers of Monitoring Devices, Communication Infrastructure Providers and Analytical Software Providers to design and configure end-to-end solution in the areas of “Condition Monitoring” of the Electrical Assets. Combining deep industry experience and technical knowhow with creative ideas, NCEPL has created intelligent, reliable and leading-edge technology solutions that add value to our customers’ businesses and help them to move to a “Condition Based Maintenance Program” from an existing “Time Based Maintenance Program”.
